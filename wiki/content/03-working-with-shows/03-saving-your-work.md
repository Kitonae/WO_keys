## Saving Your Work

### Save Commands

- **Save** (`Ctrl+S`) – Save to current location

- **Save As** – Save with a new name or location

- **Save Copy** – Create a backup without changing current file

### Auto-Recovery

WATCHOUT automatically saves recovery data. If the application closes unexpectedly, you'll be prompted to recover your work on next launch.
