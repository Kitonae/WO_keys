## Customizing Your Workspace

WATCHOUT's interface is fully customizable to match your workflow.

### Rearranging Windows

Drag window title bars to dock them in new locations or float them as separate windows.

### Saving Layouts

Go to **Window â†’ Save Layout** to save your current arrangement. Load saved layouts from the same menu.

### Layout Presets

Use `Ctrl+Shift+1` through `Ctrl+Shift+9` to save quick-access layout presets.

### Theme

Toggle between dark and light themes from the Window menu.
