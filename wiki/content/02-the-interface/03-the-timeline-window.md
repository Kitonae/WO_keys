## The Timeline Window

The Timeline is where you orchestrate your show – arranging media and controlling precisely when things happen.

### Timeline Structure

- **Timecode Ruler** – Shows time in hours:minutes:seconds:frames

- **Layers** – Horizontal tracks for organizing content (higher layers appear on top)

- **Play Cursor** – The red vertical line indicating current playback position

### Basic Operations

- **Play/Pause** – Press `Spacebar`

- **Jump to Time** – Click in the timecode ruler

- **Zoom** – Use `Numpad +/-` or zoom controls

- **Add Layer** – Press `Ctrl+I`
