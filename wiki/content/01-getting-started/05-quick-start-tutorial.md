## Quick Start Tutorial

Follow these steps to create your first show:

### Step 1: Create a New Show

Go to **File → New** or press `Ctrl+N`.

### Step 2: Add a Display

Go to **Stage → Add Display**. This creates a display area representing your output device.

### Step 3: Import Media

Drag image or video files directly into the **Assets** window, or use **Media → Add Media File**.

### Step 4: Add Media to Timeline

Drag your media from the Assets window onto the Timeline. Position it on a layer at the desired time.

### Step 5: Position on Stage

With the cue selected, drag it on the Stage window to position it on your display.

### Step 6: Preview

Press **Spacebar** to play your timeline and preview the result.

### Step 7: Save

Go to **File → Save** or press `Ctrl+S` to save your show.
