## The Stage Window

The Stage is your visual canvas – a representation of all your displays and how content appears on them.

### Navigation

- **Pan** – Hold `Ctrl+Alt` and drag

- **Zoom** – Use `Ctrl+Mouse Wheel` or zoom buttons

- **Frame All** – Press `Ctrl+Shift+D` to fit all displays in view

### View Modes

- **Default** – Standard 2D editing view

- **Perspective** – 3D view for mapping projects

- **Projector View** – See content from projector's perspective

### Working with Content

Select, move, and transform media cues directly on the Stage. Use handles to resize and rotate.
