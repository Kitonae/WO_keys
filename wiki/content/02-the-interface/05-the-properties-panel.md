## The Properties Panel

The Properties panel shows settings for whatever is currently selected – displays, cues, or media.

### Accessing Properties

- Select an item and view its properties automatically

- Double-click an item to open detailed settings

- Press `Enter` with an item selected

### Common Properties

- **Position** – X, Y coordinates on stage

- **Scale** – Size as percentage

- **Rotation** – Rotation in degrees

- **Opacity** – Transparency level
